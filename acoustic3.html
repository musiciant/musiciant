Product Details


Classical 4/4-sized guitar from 200 series that features a top plate made of resonant Sitka spruce and body and sides made of exotic Nato wood (from mora tree). The neck is made of Jabon wood and is reinforced with rosewood rods, and fitted with a fingerboard made of ebonized mahogany with 19 brass frets and side dot inlays. Scale length is 650 mm, nut width is 52 mm. The instrument further features an ebonized maple bridge, black ABS binding on the top plate and the neck, Jabon binding on the sides and nickel-plated vintage tuners. Antique Natural colour design.
