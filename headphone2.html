Product Details


Conceived and designed by our team at Orange Amplification in the UK, the ‘O’ Edition headphone is the first product to be developed for the company’s recently created Consumer Division.

For almost fifty years Orange amplifiers have been creating a highly respected musical heritage, in the hands of some of the most revered and respected artists in music history.

The ‘O’ Edition headphones capture the very essence of Orange. We have created a rich, superb-sounding headphone with a unique and elegant aesthetic. Etched into brushed stainless steel is the iconic Orange Crest, which signifies our unwavering commitment to quality – a pledge which has been a hallmark of our brand since we first opened the doors to the famous Orange music shop in London’s West End in 1968.

• Style On Ear | Closed Back
• Driver Size 40mm
• Sensitivity 116DB (1KHZ 0.5VRMS)
• Impedance 32 Ohms
• Frequency Response 20Hz – 20KHz
• Connectivity Wired with Smartphone compatibility
