About Musiciant

Musiciant is a partnership online music store established by
Musiciant Co., a music-focused company owned and operated
by Kyle Rodriguez and Angelou Salvador in the Philippines.
The store offers a variety of high-quality music appliances
and albums suitable for teens, young adults and simply for any
fan of music. The trend for albums (especially K-Pop and Western ones)
has been undeniably growing over the past year. However, music is for
everyone to revel in and Musiciant is here to fulfill
that entertainment.

In the Philippines, reliable online music appliance stores
are limited. This is one of the reasons why the company was formed
for pursuing the idea of opening an online store and also because
of the creators' dreams to connect more people to music.

Musiciant aims to be the best music appliance and
musical instruments store in the Philippines.




About The Creators

Kyle Mc Leandrey Rodriguez

Creator


As a patient of acute bronchial asthma, Kyle grew up around computers
and musical instruments, both becoming the center of his hobbies. His
aspirations were technology-centric when he was younger, but he began
to love music more as he grew up and considered it as one of his
career paths. Stepping into college led him to be more practical,
taking Computer Science as his bachelor course. However, regardless
of what road he is driving his way onto right now, he is certain of
one thing: he will never give up music, and he would still aim to
be a musician in his lifetime, whenever that may be in the future.

Angelou Salvador

Creator


Born and raised in a household full of love for music, Angelou learned
to play the flute at the age of 7. She grew up listening to a variety
of music genres, and until now, she kept on exploring. She didn't
pursue a college degree related to music and instead chose a degree in
which she thought would help nourish her career in the future.
Music has always been a source of entertainment and her means to
relax during pastimes. It will always be a part of her life and she
aims to expand her reach to connect people to music by utilizing her
knowledge.
